Case Studies • Royal Caribbean Group • Fleet Refit

Re-platforming the Minibar Across the Solstice Class.

Royal Caribbean Group has awarded DFWIN the minibar program for the refurbishment of its Celebrity Solstice-class ships, beginning spring 2026. A category that touches every cabin, on every sailing, for the next decade.

The Hardware Solution

The custom-spec DFWIN Minibar features high-durability thermal components and low-noise absorption systems, engineered specifically for cabinet-fit integrations in luxury cruise staterooms. Tested for marine salt air environments and continuous operation.

01 / The Brief

Exacting Fleet Standards

A refurbishment program of the Solstice class is a fleet-wide event. Five ships, thousands of cabins, sailing schedules that don't bend, and a guest expectation set by the rest of a five-star cruise experience. Royal Caribbean Group needed a partner who could supply the unit at premium quality and consistent spec across the entire class—and back the program through commissioning, refit windows, and the years of warranty support that follow.

02 / What We Did

Direct Sourcing DNA

DFWIN already had the cruise-line minibar category in its DNA. We brought engineering, sourcing, and onboard support to the table as a single offer: a fridge specified for marine conditions, factory-validated against the operator's own QA standard, packed and routed for delivery to the yard on the dry-dock window. The award is the result of a multi-year qualification cycle—sample evaluation, on-site factory audits, and reference checks.

03 / The Result

Accountable Execution

Starting spring 2026, every Solstice-class refurbishment slot will be supplied with DFWIN minibar fridges. The contract represents a strategic shift for the operator—moving the category onto a single, accountable supply chain that can be coordinated across yards in Singapore, the Bahamas, and Europe.

Why It Matters

"This is the kind of win that doesn't come from a brochure. It comes from twelve years of working the cruise-line minibar category—knowing what fails in service, what surveyors flag, and how to land a container at a yard the day before a refit slot opens. The Solstice award is a vote of confidence in that operational fluency."
